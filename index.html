Regina Bistecca

Located in Florence (Italy):Just steps from the Duomo di Firenze, Regina Bistecca is an elegant restaurant that offers a modern spin of traditional Tuscan cuisine. The menu features a wide variety antipasti and sides to accompany the main attraction.

Favourite Dish: Spaghetti & Meatballs.

  • Service: 8/10 Service took a little bit longer than expected, but the staff is very kind.
  • Cleanliness: 10/10 The place was absolutely spotless.
  • Presentation: 8/10 Such a beautiful dish!
  • Flavour: 10/10 It had multiple flavours dancing on my tongue.
  • Over All: 9/10 It was a wonderful experience I totally recommend Regina Bistecca if you're visiting Italy.

Bistrot Instinct

Located in Paris (France):This pocket handkerchief contemporary bistro is in the hands of chef Maximilian Wollek and his enthusiastic youthful team. A market-fresh culinary score and updated classics with bistronomic overtones.

Favourite Dish: Marinated White Tuna.

  • Service: 10/10 The service was absolutely briliant, we didn't have to wait long, staff always checked up on us and there was entertainment while we waited.
  • Cleanliness: 10/10 Not a crumb, smudge of sauce, or dirt in general.
  • Presentation: 7/10 For a 5 star restaurant I had expected better but it did look good considering it was home-made.
  • Flavour: 7/10 It did taste delicious but i feel like the pepper over powered all the other flavours.
  • Over All: 8.5/10 It was a wonderful experience I totally recommend Bistrot Instinct if you find yourself in Paris.

Jesus é Goês

Located in Lisbon (Portugal):Spice-loving visitors to Lisbon will appreciate the enduring culinary connections between Portugal and its former colony, Goa. There are a handful of Goan restaurants in the city, of which Jesus é Goês (‘Jesus is Goan’) is particularly well-loved and with reason.

Favourite Dish: Shrimp samoosas

  • Service: 10/10 The service was awesome all workers had a smile on their faces
  • Cleanliness: 7/10 There was a few crumbs on my table but it wasn't that bad
  • Presentation: 9/10 The presentation was amazing, the dipping sauce was beautifully spread in the side portion of the plate
  • Flavour: 10/10 I never knew what to expect but it was delicious the coriander dipping sauce was the cherry on the top for me.
  • Over All: 9/10 It was a wonderful experience I totally recommend Jesus é Goês if you find yourself in Portagal.
